Biography

Birth Date: July 30, 1947

Father: Gustav Schwarzenegger

Mother: Aurelia Jadrny

Education: University of Wisconsin - Superior, B.A., 1980

Arnold Schwarzenegger was born in Austria and became one of the greatest bodybuilders of all time. His dedication and hard work led him to a successful career in Hollywood and politics.
